This worksheet covers eigenvalue theory in depth. It is designed to accompany Tutorial 4 of the Linear Algebra course and should take approximately 90 minutes to complete.

## Topics Covered

- Finding eigenvalues via the characteristic polynomial
- Computing eigenvectors for each eigenvalue
- Algebraic and geometric multiplicity
- Conditions for diagonalizability
- Diagonalization procedure and verification
- Application: computing matrix powers efficiently

## Before You Begin

Make sure you are comfortable with:
- Determinants (covered in Tutorial 2)
- Matrix multiplication and inverse
- The concept of linear independence
