Practice problems on single-qubit operations. These problems are designed to build fluency with gate matrices, state transformations, and circuit composition.

## Problem Set Contents

1. Pauli gates (X, Y, Z) — matrix form and geometric interpretation
2. The Hadamard gate and superposition
3. Phase gates (S, T) and their effect on the Bloch sphere
4. Gate composition and matrix multiplication
5. Measuring states after gate sequences
6. Challenge: design a circuit that maps |0⟩ to a given target state

## Tips

- Always check your work by computing ⟨ψ|ψ⟩ = 1 (normalisation)
- Visualise operations on the Bloch sphere using the provided diagrams
- The worked solutions at the back cover problems 1–3 only
