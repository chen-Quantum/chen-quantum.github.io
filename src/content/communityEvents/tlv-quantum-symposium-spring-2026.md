The first annual Tel Aviv Quantum Computing Student Symposium — a full day of student presentations, research posters, and panel discussions on the future of quantum science and technology in Israel.

## Programme

- Morning: Research presentations (15 min each + Q&A)
- Lunch: Poster session and networking
- Afternoon: Panel discussion — "Careers in Quantum: Academia, Industry, and Startups"
- Closing keynote by invited senior researcher

## Call for Submissions

We welcome abstracts on any topic in quantum computing, quantum information, or related areas from students at any Israeli university. Submissions are reviewed by the organising committee.

## Registration

Free to attend — registration required. Lunch provided.

This event is made possible through the generous support of the TAU Quantum Information Center and our industry partners.
