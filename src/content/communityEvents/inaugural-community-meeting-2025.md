The first gathering of what would become the TAU Quantum Community — a student-led initiative to connect, learn, and grow within the quantum computing ecosystem.

We opened with a short presentation on the global state of quantum technology and why building a local student community matters. The evening included:

- Introductions and community vision
- A 30-minute lecture on quantum advantage
- Open discussion: what should we build together?
- Networking and coffee

More than 40 students attended from CS, Physics, and Mathematics departments. The energy in the room set the tone for everything that followed.
