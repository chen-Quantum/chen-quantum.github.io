A six-hour hands-on workshop for students and researchers who want to go beyond theory and write real quantum programs.

## What We Covered

- Introduction to the Qiskit SDK
- Building quantum circuits from scratch
- Simulating circuits on the Qiskit Aer simulator
- Running circuits on real IBM quantum hardware
- Grover's search algorithm — from circuit to result
- Noise, errors, and NISQ-era constraints

## Who Attended

30 participants from across the university — physics, computer science, and mathematics students as well as two faculty members.

## Outcome

Every participant left with a working Qiskit environment and at least one circuit they designed and ran themselves. The workshop materials are available to community members.
